# DFreds Droppables

**Version:** 5.3.0
**Used In:** Agnostic-Dev 
**Purpose:** Enables GMs to drag and drop rich content (tokens, tiles, audio, journal entries, lights, and more) directly onto the canvas from folders or compendiums.

- ✅ **Enable Canvas Drag/Upload**
